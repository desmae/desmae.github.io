New Features

Update: Alpha 6

Finally, here comes Alpha 6! This is a huge update! Here are the new and exciting features:

  • Majora's Mask Support is now here! The bot now supports Majora's Mask, and we will be making sure it is as fun and engaging as Ocarina of Time with your help!
    Check out the commands for Majora's Mask here!
  • Complete item guessing rewrite! The item guessing system has been completely rewritten to be rarity-based! (Common, Uncommon, Rare... etc). Higher rarities tend to give more points!
  • OBS UI item update! With the new guessing system, the on-screen overlay now displays the item rarity as a color (Common -> Gray, Uncommon -> Green)
  • This website now shows all previous Top 5 game winners on the front page. If you'd like your name to be blurred out let me know at any time in my comments!
  • Added tips! Tips will now be displayed in the on-screen display to help new users occasionally and provide cool information.
  • Endgame rework! Endgame has been reworked to be compatible with the new rarity system. During Endgame, Very Common items can be used in streaks (originally they cannot), and Epic and Legendary items are worth double!
  • !viewtext lets you review what your win text is!
  • !myscore lets you see what your current score is!
  • !myguess now shows you not only your current guess, but your current dungeon and boss guess!
  • !mystreak shows your current streak in case you don't know!
  • Player streaks, boss guesses, dungeon guesses are now properly tracked on the backend and persist between streams!
  • More shortcuts: !myscore = !ms, !myguess = !mg, and more! Check the command list for more information
  • Compasses and Dungeon Maps are now back as guessable items worth 10 pts for specific guesses and 5 pts for general guesses.
  • Auto-lock-in now should properly work!
  • Dungeon and boss guesses now take streaks into account!
