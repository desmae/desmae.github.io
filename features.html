Update: Alpha 4

Here comes Alpha 4! This update introduces a couple of new features and improvements to enhance your experience:

  • Song Teleport Randomization! Allows you to guess what OoT song will lead to which location. See its use here!
  • Message updates... The chat bot will now send additional messages properly instead of processing them and not providing feedback sometimes.
  • Win Screen!!! At the end of the randomizer, the top three players will be displayed on screen, with the player in first having special text (!settext Yippee! I won!)
  • The item list on this website is updated and has a better UI! Use the search bar to look for specific items!
  • Tied Leaderboard Positions! If you're sharing first place with someone, you both get a gold outline!!!
  • Streak indicator for the leaderboard! Show off your guessing skills with a visible fire icon next to your name on the leaderboard!
  • Fixed some bugs...
  • Backend optimizations
